ObiiNext - Introducing service vertical of Obii Kriationz

Balaji Sanjit

Client Relationship Manager

May 23, 2022
Facebook Twitter LinkedIn Mail

We are delighted to introduce ObiiNext.com - our standalone service vertical catering to Web Development, Software Development, SEO services, Cloud Consulting, UI/UX consulting and DevOps consulting.

ObiiNext is an attempt to rehash and re-energise our service vertical and consciously have a dedicated team of product managers and software engineers to cater to our growing list of customers.

ObiiNext envisions delivering next-generation tech solutions in line with the notion set forward by us at Obii Kriationz Web LLP. Located in Bangalore (Bengaluru) and Da Nang, Vietnam, ObiiNext houses an excellent team of product managers, expert engineering teams and cloud consultants.

ObiiNext's logo emphasises a cultural shift, a strategic move from our status quo to a dynamic future. Colours represent a realistic outlook with proactive dynamism.

ObiiNext is a digital transformation engine. Digital transformation is a paradigm-altering change in an organisation's principles, policies, and procedures by building digital assets. In this Digital age, A company must undergo a radical transition to break the cycle of slow expansion and accelerate. Even successful companies of today must focus on the needs of future generations while also keeping up with the latest technological advancements. ObiiNext provides the perfect recipe for digital adoption.

ObiiNext.com is built on WordPress with a specific Elementor-based, lightweight theme.
